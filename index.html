What's Going On?

Click anywhere to set off ripple effect and sound
The pitch will change depending on where you click (highest note @ top right and lowest note @ bottom left)
Choose your sound with the buttons on the bottom right
Click the to open settings menu
The numbers on the bottom left are the fibonacci sequence number and the fibonacci number itself reading top to bottom
Once you reach the 7th fibonacci number you unlock the drop down menus below the square
Use the drop down menus and the "Commence" button to change colors and animate the square
Once you reach the 34th fibonacci number, you win and unlock a special sound, and subsequently become more popular
Once you unlock both special sounds, you unlock "Free Mode" accessible from the settings menu. Free Mode removes the fibonacci sequence and allows you to click to your hearts content
The reset button in the settings menu starts you back at "square one"...
A link to the code is hidden somewhere on the page
